Relago is Latin for: to bring back a message, refer. It is a client for the Read It Later web service.

Save: Save pages from your computer or phone

Read on or offline: Read at home, work, on the plane, or during your commute; even without an internet connection

Access Anywhere: Whether you want to read it from your phone or your desktop, you can.



Using Relego to save your webpages for later reading works great. But who wants to copy and paste in the URL every time?

To remedy this, we have created a special patch for your webOS device. This patch gives you the ability to use the browser on your device as normal, then send the URL directly to Relego with a few taps. After installing the patch, simply tap on the Application Menu, then Page, and "Add to Relego".



Getting the Patch
If you are a Preware user, simply load up the patches section. Go into the Browser category and install the 'Send to Relego' patch.
